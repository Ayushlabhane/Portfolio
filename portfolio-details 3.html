Mobile Sales Analysis

Project information

  • Tool Used : Excel, MYSQL, Power BI
  • Project URL : Live Interactive Dashboard
About this Project :-

The Mobile Sales Analytics Dashboard is an interactive business intelligence project designed to analyze mobile sales performance across cities, brands, age groups, and payment methods. The dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of revenue, units sold, pricing trends, and customer demographics to support data-driven sales strategy decisions.

Key Insights :-
  1. Total revenue generated is 769M with 19K units sold.
  2. Mid-age customers contribute the highest share of total revenue.
  3. Delhi and Mumbai lead in units sold among major cities.
  4. Apple, Samsung, and OnePlus hold the highest brand revenue contribution (~20% each).
  5. UPI and Card payments dominate over cash transactions.
  6. Monthly sales remain relatively stable, averaging around 1.5K–1.8K units per month.
Business Recommendation :-
  1. Focus marketing campaigns on mid-age customer segments for higher revenue impact.
  2. Strengthen inventory and promotional efforts in high-performing cities like Delhi and Mumbai.
  3. Promote high-margin models while monitoring price sensitivity trends across brands.
  4. Encourage digital payment incentives to further increase UPI and card transactions.
