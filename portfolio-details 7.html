About this Project :-

Cricket analytics has become an important part of modern sports strategy. The IPL Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) project focuses on analyzing historical IPL match and delivery data to uncover performance trends, team strengths, player contributions, and match dynamics. Using Python-based data analysis techniques, raw ball-by-ball match data was processed and visualized to extract insights about scoring patterns, match outcomes, and team performances across IPL seasons.

Key Insights :-
  1. Analysis performed on match-level and ball-by-ball delivery data to understand scoring patterns and match results.
  2. Identified teams with the highest win counts and strongest performance across seasons.
  3. Discovered top run-scoring batsmen and highest wicket-taking bowlers based on delivery-level data.
  4. Analyzed toss decisions and their impact on match outcomes, highlighting strategic advantages.
  5. Identified high-scoring venues and stadiums where teams tend to score more runs.
  6. Studied run distribution across overs to understand scoring acceleration during middle and death overs.
  7. Identified teams with strongest chasing performance vs teams that perform better when defending totals.
Business/Analytical Conclusion :-
  1. Teams winning the toss tend to prefer chasing in certain stadium conditions.
  2. Certain venues consistently produce higher scoring matches, indicating pitch characteristics favorable for batting.
  3. Top-performing batsmen significantly influence team win probability, highlighting the importance of star players.
  4. Strategic bowling during death overs plays a major role in controlling match outcomes.
