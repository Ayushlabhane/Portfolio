About this Project :-

The Internet Usage Dashboard is an interactive analytics project designed to analyze screen time patterns across different age groups, device types, and internet sources. The dashboard provides a clear breakdown of productive vs leisure usage, demographic distribution, and monthly usage trends to understand digital behavior patterns.

Key Insights :-
  • Total screen time recorded is 22.8K hours, with productive (11.2K) and leisure (11.5K) usage nearly balanced.
  • Age group 10–30 years contributes the highest overall screen time.
  • Productive usage dominates compared to entertainment and social media individually.
  • WiFi and Mobile Data usage is almost evenly distributed.
  • Mobile devices contribute the largest share of total usage compared to Laptop and Tablet.
  • Screen time remains relatively stable month-wise, ranging between 1.8K–2.1K hours.
Business / Analytical Conclusion :-
  • Younger age groups are the most digitally active and drive maximum engagement.
  • Mobile-first behavior indicates strong dependency on smartphones for both work and leisure.
  • Balanced productive and leisure usage suggests blended digital consumption patterns.
