About this Project :-

In the competitive landscape of e-commerce, analyzing sales and profitability is essential for making strategic business decisions. The E-commerce Sales Performance Dashboard is designed to analyze sales performance, profit trends, customer behavior, and product category performance.

Key Insights :-
  1. Total 37K profit generated from 438K total sales amount across 5615 total orders.
  2. Printers and Bookcases are the most profitable sub-categories compared to other products.
  3. Clothing category contributes the highest share (~63%) of total quantity sold.
  4. Cash on Delivery (COD) is the most used payment mode (~44%), followed by UPI and Debit Card.
  5. Maharashtra contributes the highest sales amount, followed by Madhya Pradesh and Uttar Pradesh.
  6. Monthly profit analysis shows profit fluctuations with strong performance in early and late months of the year.
Business Recommendation :-
  1. Focus marketing and inventory planning on high-profit sub-categories like printers and bookcases.
  2. Promote digital payment methods such as UPI and cards to reduce dependency on Cash on Delivery.
  3. Strengthen sales strategies in high-performing states like Maharashtra to maximize revenue growth.
  4. Analyze low-profit months to identify operational or seasonal factors affecting profitability.
